Appleby Frodingham Ladies Hockey Club

About Us

We are a small friendly club which can trace its origins back to the early 1920's based in Scunthorpe, North Lincolnshire.
At present, we run only one Ladies team and for the season 2023/2024 we will be playing in the Yorkshire & North East - Wold Women's Division 1 following the reorganisation of the league structures by England Hockey.
All of our home games are at Brumby Hall Sports Ground, Scunthorpe. The sand-based pitch is a hockey-specific facility and forms part of a major development of the outdoor playing facilities by Appleby Frodingham Works Athletic Club. We are always looking for new players of all ages (13yrs+) and ability.
So, if you've not picked up your hockey stick in a while, you've just moved into the area or just want to come and play please use our Contact page to send us a message and we'll get back to you ASAP.
